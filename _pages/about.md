I am a psychologist (M.Sc.) and research associate in the <a href='https://www.psychology.hu-berlin.de/en/prof/emmy/kompmod/kompmod'> Computational Modeling Lab at HU Berlin</a> under the supervision of <a href='https://sites.google.com/site/fritzgntr/home'>Prof. Dr. Fritz Günther</a>. My research centers around <i>computational cognitive science</i>, <i>machine learning</i>, and <i>metascience</i>. I am particularly interested in the development of behavioral methods to improve the understanding of large language models.

If you want to get in touch, please email me at <b>louis.schiekiera [at] hu-berlin.de</b>.
