Excited to share that our paper _From Associations to Activations: Comparing Behavioral and Hidden-State Semantic Geometry in LLMs_ has been **accepted at ICML 2026**! Across eight instruction-tuned LLMs and 17.5M+ trials, we show that forced-choice behavior recovers hidden-state semantic geometry far better than free association.

*Paper:* [arXiv:2602.00628](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.00628) · *Code:* [GitHub](https://github.com/schiekiera/llm-association-geometry) · *Data:* [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/schiekiera/llm-association-geometry). *TLDR:* [Blog post](/blog/2026/neural-semantic-geometry/).
