New preprint! _A harmonized trial-level dataset of picture-word interference_ — 688,976 trials from 86 experiments across 42 studies (3,353 English- and German-speaking participants), assembled from five sources and unified into a single 28-variable schema, with the full cleaning pipeline released alongside the data.

*Preprint:* [PsyArXiv](https://osf.io/preprints/psyarxiv/xp69t_v1) · *Data & pipeline:* [OSF (10.17605/OSF.IO/2B3SX)](https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/2B3SX) · *TLDR:* [Blog post](/blog/2026/pwi-dataset/).
